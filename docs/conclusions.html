Conclusions

The current literature recognizes the relevance of commodities prices movements for small open economies. The terms of trade (approximated by ratio of export price index and the import price index) have been considered one of the main drivers in the observed growth for countries like Chile, and Peru in recent years (both mining exporters) with a outstanding copper price due China's developing process.
