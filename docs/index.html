Global Shocks

Heterogeneous Effects in Small Open Economies

In the literature it is well known that commodity prices movements have a sizable impact in domestic aggregates for Small Open Economies. In fact, many economists build models that take them as a sufficient statistics for the rest of the world. In this research, I suggest a novel identification for global shocks and compare with a standard shock based only on commodities prices. XXXXXXXX
